Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you provide home service for motor repair?

Yes! We offer FREE pickup and delivery service for motor rewinding and appliance repairs across Patna. Call us on 8347588525 to schedule a pickup.

What are your working hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday, 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM. For emergency motor repairs, call us 24/7 on 8347588525. Sunday visits can be arranged on request.

Do you offer warranty on repairs?

Absolutely! All motor rewinding services come with a 6-month warranty. For new products, we provide manufacturer's warranty (typically 1-2 years depending on brand).

Which brands do you stock?

We are authorized dealers for premium brands: Polycab, Havells, Crompton, Bajaj, Orient, Syska, and more. All products are 100% genuine with manufacturer warranty.

Do you accept bulk orders for B2B?

Yes! We welcome B2B partnerships and bulk orders for contractors, builders, and factories. Special wholesale rates available. Contact us for a custom quote.

How long does motor rewinding take?

Most motors are repaired within 24-48 hours. For urgent cases, we offer same-day service (charges may apply). Complex industrial motors may take 2-3 days.
