Choosing the Right Wire Gauge for Your Home

Nov 05, 2024 Ashish Singh

Using thin wires for heavy appliances is the #1 cause of short circuits in Patna. Let's simplify which wire size you actually need.

Standard Wire Sizes Chart

Wire Size (sq mm) Ampacity Best Use Case
0.75 mm² 6 Amps LED Bulbs, Fancy Lights, Earth Wire (Internal)
1.5 mm² 12-14 Amps Fans, TVs, Laptops, Mobile Chargers
2.5 mm² 18-20 Amps Washing Machines, Iron, Kitchen Sockets
4.0 mm² 25-30 Amps AC (1.5 Ton), Geysers, Microwaves
6.0 mm² 35-40 Amps Main Distribution Board Input (Sub-mains)

Why Brands Matter?

Local wires often use impure copper (alloy) which has higher resistance and heats up quickly. Always look for ISI Marked wires from reputable brands like Polycab, Havells, or RR. They use 99.9% electrolytic grade copper.
