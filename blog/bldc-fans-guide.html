How BLDC Fans Save 65% Electricity

Oct 28, 2024 Ashish Singh

In Bihar, where electricity bills can spike during summer, switching to BLDC fans is one of the smartest investments. But what exactly is BLDC technology?

What is BLDC?

BLDC stands for Brushless Direct Current. Unlike traditional induction motors that use carbon brushes and consume 70-80 watts, BLDC motors use electronic commutation and permanent magnets. This allows them to run efficiently on just 28-35 watts at full speed.

The Savings Calculation

Let's do the math for a typical household with 3 fans running 12 hours a day:

  • Ordinary Fan: 75W x 3 fans x 12 hrs = 2.7 Units/Day
  • BLDC Fan: 28W x 3 fans x 12 hrs = 1 Unit/Day
  • Daily Saving: 1.7 Units
  • Yearly Saving: ~600 Units x ₹7/unit = ₹4,200 Saved!

Key Benefits

Remote Controlled

Control speed, sleep timer, and boost mode from your bed.

Inverter Friendly

Triples your battery backup duration during power cuts.
