Our Journey

Balaji Electronics began its journey in 2012 as a dedicated repair shop in Parsa, Bihar. Founded by Mr. Ashish Kumar Singh, the vision was clear from day one: to provide honest, high-quality electrical repairs without ever cutting corners.

Over the last 12 years, that small shop has evolved into one of the region's most trusted independent service centers. Today, we specialize in industrial motors and home electrical solutions, bridging the gap between traditional technical expertise and modern service standards.
